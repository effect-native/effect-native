# MiniDom Open Issues

## Anti-Bias Protocol
1. Capture every new assumption as a hypothesis written in the negative.
2. Design a disproof experiment before acting on the hypothesis.
3. Record evidence from the experiment and update the specs; if evidence goes stale, reopen the hypothesis.

## Current Status
- No open hypotheses or unresolved issues at this time.
- Re-run the protocol whenever a new backend, capability, or performance requirement emerges.
